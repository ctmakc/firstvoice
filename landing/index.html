The Solution

Three pillars that make FirstVoice unlike any other preservation platform.

🗣️

AI Speech Technology

Fine-tuned Whisper ASR for low-resource languages. Gemini-powered translation to English and French. Coqui TTS voice cloning so the language can "speak" again.

⛓️

Web3 Provenance

Soulbound NFTs on Polygon prove origin without speculation. Tamper-proof, censorship-resistant, community-owned attribution. No crypto hype — just immutable proof.

🔒

Sacred Governance

OCAP-by-design: communities decide what is public, what is sacred, who accesses it. Technical enforcement — not just policy documents. AI training gated per-item.
