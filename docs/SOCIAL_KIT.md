**Post 2 — Technical Deep Dive (3 days later)**
```
How do you build AI speech technology for a language with < 10 hours of training data?

FirstVoice uses three techniques for ultra-low-resource ASR:

1. Curriculum learning: sacred → community-review → public (quality ladder)
2. Cross-lingual transfer: English/Māori bilingual prompts guide Whisper
3. Community-validated ground truth: elder-approved transcriptions as gold standard

The result: practical community use, not lab benchmarks.

But the real innovation is governance. Every recording has:
• Visibility: Sacred / Community-Review / Public
• AI training opt-in: per-item, not blanket
• Provenance: Soulbound NFT on Polygon (non-transferable, zero speculation)

This is what "AI for good" looks like when the community is in the driver's seat.

→ https://github.com/ctmakc/firstvoice

#MachineLearning #LowResourceNLP #SpeechRecognition #IndigenousAI #EthicalAI
```
