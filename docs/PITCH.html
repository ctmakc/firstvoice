The Solution

🎙️ Record

Elders record stories in-browser. Offline-first PWA — works on any phone without app install.

🧠 Understand

AI transcribes in the source language and translates with cultural annotations. Whisper + Gemini.

🔒 Govern

Community council decides: public, sacred, or restricted. AI training gated per-item. Blockchain provenance.

OCAP-by-Design

Ownership, Control, Access, Possession — not policy documents, but technical guarantees.

Principle Technical Enforcement
Ownership Communities hold encryption keys for sacred recordings
Control Per-recording visibility: Sacred / Community-Review / Public
Access Elder key authentication — council members only
Possession Self-hostable MinIO — data stays in community jurisdiction
